2.2. Log out of SAW

 

At the upper right corner of the screen select the ICON to access Account Settings.  This icon differs (letter and color) depending on the  user

 

How to Log Out of Saw

  1. Click on the ICON from the upper right-hand corner of the screen
  2. Click on Log Out

 

Figure 2 Logout and Account Setting for Admin

 

NOTE: The Account Settings selections for a Non-System Admin will appear without the ‘Admin’ as a choice for selection.
