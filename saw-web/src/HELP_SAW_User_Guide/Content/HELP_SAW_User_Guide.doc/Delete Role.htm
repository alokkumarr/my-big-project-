How to Delete a Role

Prior to a Role being deleted the privileges that have been created for that Role must be removed! (Refer to 4.4.4. Delete a Privilege,

  1. From the Role Management screen (refer to 4.3.1. Access Role Management
  1. Click on the Delete icon () on the row of the Role to be deleted

  1. Click on Delete to proceed with the role deletion or Cancel to terminate the deletion
